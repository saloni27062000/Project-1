Come across a glitch on Doctor360?

We are very appreciative of you getting in touch. Kindly we ask that alongside letting us know you have encountered an error, that as much information is provided as possible in regards to what you are encountering, this helps our team get onto investigating the issue straight away.



Helpful information you can include in your form:

  • Whether you are using a phone or on the Doctor360 website
  • If on the website, what browser you are using to access Doctor360
  • If you are on a phone, what model of phone you are on
  • If you are on a phone, what version of the Doctor360 app you have installed
  • Screenshots of what is occurring ( if possible)
  • This assists our team to have all the necessary information so they can get straight into solving the problem for you!

Please note we are not a medical service and cannot offer advice nor assist with any medical ailments. If you are needing medical attention or advice urgently please call your nearest emergency services or search Doctor360 for a practice near you.
